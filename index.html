Frequently Asked Questions

To enroll in a course, simply browse the available courses, click on the course you’re interested in, and select the "Enroll" button. You will be prompted to create an account or log in if you haven’t already.

Some courses may have prerequisites, which will be listed in the course description. Be sure to review the course requirements before enrolling.

Refund policies vary by course and institution. Please check the course-specific refund policy before enrolling. Typically, refunds are only offered within the first few days after enrollment.

You can send messages to your instructor or fellow students through the platform’s messaging feature. Simply navigate to the "Messages" section in your dashboard.
